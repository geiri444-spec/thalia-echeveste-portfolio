About

Crafting characters
that live on screen

Thalía Echeveste is a Makeup SFX, Wigs & Hair, and Tattoo designer based in Mexico, with over a decade of experience in film, television, and advertising. Trained in wig making in London, she brings an international precision to every project — from intimate character work to large-scale special effects.

Her work spans award-nominated features, major streaming productions, and high-profile editorial campaigns. Known for her meticulous approach and deep understanding of character, Thalía creates looks that are not just technically flawless, but narratively essential.
